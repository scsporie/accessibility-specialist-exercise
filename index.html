Accessibility Capture The Flag

Welcome to the Accessibility Team's Capture the Flag event. The goal of this event is to give you an idea of where you stand, in regards to your awareness of accessibility standards. Your objective is to evaluate how well the website linked below fits WCAG's accessibility standards. If you find any issues, note them. A link is provided with some tools that you can use to evaluate the page. Submit the finished product to the Accessibility Team. Click the link below to access the website.
